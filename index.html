Board of Councilors

Each orthopaedic surgeon who serves on the Board of Councilors acts as your representative and liaison to the Academy. A Councilor's goal is to represent your opinions, ideas, suggestions, and seek input on issues that affect orthopaedic surgeons and their patients. Each Councilor is responsible for taking comments and suggestions and presenting them at one of their three meetings and encouraging the Academy to take action.

The Board of Councilors has played active roles in encouraging the Academy to pursue public advocacy efforts and have help to refine the algorithms review process allowing more input and comments on the algorithms. Your Councilor needs input on issues that are important to you. Without input and participation from the fellows, there is no guarantee that your voice will be heard.
